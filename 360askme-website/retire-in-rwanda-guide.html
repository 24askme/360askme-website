1. Why Retire in Rwanda?

Rwanda is not just a place to grow old. It is a place to live fully, with dignity, safety, and purpose. More retirees are discovering this small, remarkable nation — and choosing to spend their golden years here with 360ASKME as their trusted partner.

What Makes Rwanda Different?

  • Safety above all: Kigali is consistently ranked as one of the safest cities in Africa.
  • Clean and green: Rwanda is famously clean. Plastic bags are banned. Streets are swept daily.
  • Political stability: Rwanda is one of Africa's most stable and well-governed nations.
  • English-speaking: English is widely spoken, making integration easy.
  • Affordable quality of life: Your pension goes further without sacrificing comfort.
  • Aging with purpose: Rwanda offers opportunities to stay active — volunteering, mentoring, or starting a small business.

2. Rwanda Retirement Visa — Long-Stay Options

Rwanda offers several long-stay visa options for retirees. 360ASKME can handle the entire application process for you.

Long-Term Visa (Retirement Visa)

  • Duration: Up to 5 years, renewable
  • Requirements: Proof of sufficient funds (pension, savings, or investments), clean criminal record, health insurance
  • Processing time: 2-4 weeks

3. Healthcare for Retirees in Rwanda

Rwanda has made remarkable progress in healthcare. Kigali offers excellent private hospitals and clinics that meet international standards.

Top Hospitals in Kigali

  • King Faisal Hospital: The premier referral hospital. Many specialists trained abroad.
  • Rwanda Military Hospital (RMH): High-quality care, modern facilities.
  • Clinique La Croix du Sud: Private clinic with European-trained doctors.
