1. Visa Requirements — Do You Need One?

Rwanda has one of the most welcoming visa policies in Africa. Citizens of all countries can obtain a visa on arrival at Kigali International Airport or at land borders. However, it is always better to prepare in advance.

Visa on Arrival

Most nationalities can get a 30-day tourist visa upon arrival. The cost is $50 USD (payable by credit card or cash).

East Africa Tourist Visa

If you plan to visit Rwanda, Kenya, and Uganda, get the East Africa Tourist Visa for $100 USD. It allows multiple entries across all three countries for 90 days.

2. Arriving at Kigali International Airport (KGL)

Kigali Airport is modern, clean, and efficient. From landing to leaving the terminal, most travelers clear immigration and baggage claim in under 45 minutes.

What to Expect

  • Free WiFi: Connect to "Kigali Airport Free WiFi" as soon as you land.
  • SIM Cards: MTN and Airtel kiosks are open for all flights.
  • Currency Exchange: There are ATMs and exchange counters in the arrivals hall.
  • Taxi Services: Official airport taxis are available 24/7.
