Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a visa to visit Rwanda? +
Yes, most nationalities get a 30‑day visa on arrival for $50 USD. We can help you prepare the documents.
Is Rwanda safe for solo travelers? +
Absolutely. Rwanda is one of the safest countries in Africa. Kigali is clean, modern, and welcoming.
How do I register a company in Rwanda? +
Online via RDB. It takes under 6 hours and is free. 360ASKME can handle the entire process for you.
What is the cost of living in Kigali? +
A comfortable lifestyle for a single person is $1,500–$2,500 per month. Rent for a 2‑bedroom apartment: $600–$1,200.
