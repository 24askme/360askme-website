# 360ASKME – Rwanda's 24/7 AI-Powered Concierge

**Stop Searching. Just Ask Me.**

360ASKME is a presidential-level platform that combines AI and human concierge services to help anyone in Rwanda find accommodation, offices, land, vehicles, professional services, and tourism experiences – all through a simple conversational interface.

🌐 **Live Site:** [https://360askme.com](https://360askme.com)

---

## How It Works

1. **User lands on the platform** – Sees two options: "Click here to ask AI" or "Click to talk to us"
2. **AI asks clarifying questions** – Budget, area, type of service
3. **AI presents options** – List of matching properties or services with prices and amenities
4. **User selects an option** – AI hands off to a human concierge
5. **Human concierge finalizes** – Booking, negotiation, payment, delivery
